About Eagledu Services LLP

Empowering Students Through Exceptional Overseas Education Guidance

Our Story

Eagledu Services LLP is one of the most trusted overseas education consultants in Hyderabad, helping students achieve their dream of studying abroad. We specialize in MS abroad, MBBS abroad, and global admissions with personalized counselling and expert guidance.

Our experienced study abroad consultants provide end-to-end support including career counselling, profile evaluation, university shortlisting, scholarship assistance, SOP and LOR preparation, visa processing, education loans, and post-departure services.

With strong partnerships with top universities in the USA, UK, Canada, Germany, Italy, Australia, Ireland, and Europe, we ensure high success rates and affordable overseas education solutions for students across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.
